Fawn

"I won't see you again, but I know you'll always be there when we need you. I'm really gonna miss you. I love you, Gruff."

Fawn is a rascally tomboy full of mirth and mischief. She’s always ready to play, and the more rough-and-tumble the game, the better. Fawn is a born prankster—she likes to play tricks on Iridessa – and Tink loves her mischievous nature. She speaks many different animal languages, and the burplike toad-speak is her favorite. In Pixie Hollow, Fawn's home is located in the bottom left corner of Maple Tree Hill, in Autumn Forest.

Did you know?

Fawn knows the scientific names of several animals, including the common opossum and an extinct species of bison.
